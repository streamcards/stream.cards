How does it work?

1. Connect your Dropbox account

Streamcards also works with

  • Google Drive
  • Amazon
  • Facebook
  • Twitter

This gives you a stream based on everything that's available.

2. Build your own stream of contents

Streamcards lets you re-shape your content by filtering and sorting

  • Text and images
  • Videos and other documents
  • User information

This gives you new streams, shaped to your particular needs.

3. Orchestrate your content using cards

Streamcards uses plugins to create new cards based on whichever content you like.

  • Lists and articles
  • Galleries and content sliders
  • Interactive forms

Tell the world your story - easy and magnificent.

4. Create beautiful pages

Streamcards comes with a powerful set of templates, which transform your stream of cards intobeautiful pages.

  • Web sites
  • Blog posts
  • Web magazines
  • What ever you imagine!

Why are we doing this?

We believe that, although there are numerous other web publishing tools out there, none of them quite fit the bill.

How's that? you may ask

We find that web publishing shold not require you to set up any infrastructure.

Instead, we believe there are so many content sharing tools out there, you certainly don't want to create what would be your own content island.

What's under the hood?

Web components

Web components are the bulding blocks of a new web.

Flow oriented programming

Understanding independent components as actors in a programmatic flow.

Reactive binding

Multiple streams talk to each other via observer/observable pattern. They react upon their actions using independent triggers and asyncronous execution.
