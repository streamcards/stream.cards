How does streamcards work?

Connect your existing accounts

Streamcards will work with Dropbox, Google Drive, Amazon, Facebook, and Twitter. Connecting to your existing services gives you a stream based on everything that's available.

Build your own stream of contents

Streamcards lets you re-shape your content by filtering and sorting text and images, videos and other documents and user information. This gives you new streams, shaped to your particular needs.

Orchestrate your content using cards

Streamcards uses plugins to create new cards based on whichever content you like. For example Lists and articles, Galleries and content sliders and Interactive forms. Tell the world your own story with streams and cards - easily and magnificent.

Create beautiful pages

Streamcards comes with a powerful set of templates, which will transform your streams of cards into beautiful pages. Go, and create websites,blog posts, web magazines and whatever you imagine.

Why are we doing this?

We believe that, although there are numerous other web publishing tools out there, none of them quite fit the bill.

»How's that?« you may ask.

We think web publishing should not require you to set up any infrastructure.

Instead, we believe there are so many content sharing tools out there, you certainly don't want to create what would be your own content island.

What's under the hood?

Web components

Web components are the bulding blocks of new web. Being reusable and highly customizable in their look and feel as well as their functionality.

Flow oriented programming

Understanding independent components as actors in a programmatic flow.

Reactive binding

Multiple streams talk to each other via observer/observable pattern. They react upon their actions using independent triggers and asyncronous execution.
